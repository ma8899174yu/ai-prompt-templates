# FLUX 美食摄影

## 效果描述

生成专业美食摄影风格的照片：菜品色泽诱人、光线恰到好处、背景简洁，能充分展现食物的质感和美味感。

## 适用场景

- 餐厅菜单设计
- 美食博主内容
- 电商食品展示
- 菜谱图书
- 餐饮品牌宣传

## 正向提示词

```
food photography, appetizing dish, top down angle,
steam rising from food, soft natural lighting, shallow depth of field,
clean white or wooden background, minimalist food styling,
gourmet magazine quality, professional food photography,
hyperdetailed, 8k resolution, photorealistic, RAW photo quality,
color graded, slight vignette
```

## 负面提示词

```
messy, cluttered, dirty dishes, spilled food,
anime, cartoon, illustration, painting, drawing,
deformed, ugly, bad anatomy, blurry, unnatural colors,
low quality, low resolution, jpeg artifacts, compression artifacts,
watermark, signature, text, logo, frames, border
```
